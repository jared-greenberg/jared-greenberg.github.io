Who I am...

Nice to meet you! My name is Jared Greenberg. I've always been a math and science kid, but something special happened when I stumbled into the Intro to Programming course in college. I was captivated, and in a few short days, I went from being undecided to coding my way through a Computer Science degree. I genuinely loved solving logic puzzles every day and turning them into tech that people could actually use. I was also passionate about music, singing with my a cappella group and performing in musical theater. That passion eventually led me to travel around the world as an actor and cruise ship singer. After a few years, I felt the desire to be more landlocked, and let's be real, to get a dog, so I began letting my college studies back into my life. I have spent the past few months intensely focusing on upgrading my skills to newer technologies and languages such as React/Redux, Javascript, Ruby on Rails, SQL, HTML5, and CSS3. Achieving my goal to be able to build full-stack applications, I coded a clone of Airbnb for renting tiny houses and piano-themed Canvas game using these skills and more (you can check out these projects below). But it won't stop there. I am eager to continue growing and to turn my passion for software engineering into a lifelong career. I am a hard worker, a quick learner, and someone who is driven by the things that bring me joy, like coding, and I look forward to beginning my next big adventure as a member of your team. And yes, I got the dog. He's a Chinese Crested named Kentucky!
